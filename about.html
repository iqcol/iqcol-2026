Our Story

Foundations & Vision

Founded in 2017, the Königsberger Bridges Institute (KBI) is an Estonian non-profit inspired by the Seven Bridges of Königsberg, a problem that sparked graph theory. We connect global innovators in AI, data science, blockchain, and trust technology, fostering skills through training, competitions, and a commitment to Sustainable Development Goals.

Official Organizer

Flagship Competitions

KBI is the official organizer of three flagship global competitions and two conferences:

  • IBCOL 2026 — International Blockchain Olympiad
  • IDSOL 2026 — International Data Science Olympiad
  • IQCOL 2026 — International Quantum Computing Olympiad
  • EETO 2026 — Eastern Emerging Technologies Olympiads
  • WETO 2026 — Western Emerging Technologies Olympiads

Over the last eight years, KBI has assisted tens of thousands of participants globally from renowned education institutions including Cornell, Cambridge, Peking University, and The University of Hong Kong, leading to the development of more than 500 incubated projects globally.
