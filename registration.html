Registration Fee

How are fees levied?

Registration fees are only collected from teams invited to the IQCOL finals. The payment details will be provided along with the invitation letter and/or visa application support letter. Fee depends on the country of representation:

  • Category A: High income.
  • Category B: Upper middle income.
  • Category C: Low and lower middle income.

Why are fees levied?

It takes monumental resources to organize the International Quantum Computing Olympiad worldwide annually. The fees levied by the organizing committee contribute to costs including food, beverages, human resources, and posterboard printing. No profits are extracted from these fees.
