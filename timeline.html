PHASE 0 JULY 2026 – AUGUST 2026

Trial Period

  • Optional but highly recommended for all participants.
  • Submit Project materials for early review by subject matter experts.
  • Receive specialized feedback and guidance.
PHASE 1 SEPTEMBER 2026 – OCTOBER 2026

Qualifier Round

  • Formal submission for official evaluation.
  • Projects undergo a rigorous technical review.
  • Results: invitation, conditional invitation, or rejection.
PHASE 2 NOVEMBER 2026 – DECEMBER 2026

Final Round

  • In-real-life event at the Host City.
  • Olympiad Exhibition: Physical posterboard presentations.
  • Olympiad Symposium: High-impact pitch presentations.
Note: IQCOL accolades are based only on scores from the Olympiad Exhibition; ETO accolades are based on Sponsors' scores from the Olympiad Symposium.
