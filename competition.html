The Olympiad

The 1st International Quantum Science Olympiad (IQCOL 2026) is a design competition where students apply first principles to propose practical use cases for Quantum Computing and Information Theory (QCIT).

Mission & Vision

The Problem

Traditional education often fails to test the complexities of real-world data application and architectural resilience.

The Solution

IQCOL 2026 provides a global stage for Quantum Computing, where engineering rigor meets creative problem-solving.

The Goal

Bridging the gap between academic theory and enterprise-ready solutions through standardized benchmarking.

Competition Categories

EXP

Exploration Proposals

Designed for advancing the states-of-the-art in QCIT through research to improve performance, scalability, and exploring new frameworks.

APP

Application Proposals

Designed for applying the states-of-the-art in QCIT across a plethora of use cases found throughout various verticals in academia, government, and industry.
